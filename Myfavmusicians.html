MY FAVARITE MUSICIAN

Sheebah Karungi

Sheebah Karungi, professional also known as Queen Karma and Sheebah, is a Ugandan Musician, Dancer, Actress and Feminist activist. She debuted her acting career in Queen of Katwe as Shakira.
Born: November 11, 1989 (age 33 years), Kawempe, Kampala
Parents: Ahamada Kimali Musoke, Edith Kabazungu
Siblings: Mariam Jumba
Also known as:Queen Karma, Sheebah
Member of: Karma music

Burna Boy
